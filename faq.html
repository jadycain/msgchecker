Will Message Checker collect my information?

No. Message Checker will never collect sensitive user information. We care about your privacy more than anything; read our Privacy Policy to know more details.

Will Message Checker get access to our conversation?

No, Message Checker doesn’t get access to any of the “conversations” from you. Instead, Only when you grant access to Message Checker can it access notifications of particular App(s) you selected. Message Checker checks risky URLs and potential misinformation and scam just for single notification content, nothing more.
